No backend. No shared database. No data broker deals. Just your Google account, your Gemini API key, and the alert channels you choose.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Does emAIl Sentinel read all my email?+

Only the Gmail labels you explicitly configure, and only the metadata + first 2,000 characters of the plain-text body. Attachment file contents are never read — only filenames. See the Privacy Policy for the full data inventory.

How fast are alerts?+

Polling is every 1, 5, 10, 15, or 30 minutes depending on your plan. Free plan is 15 minutes or slower; Pro and Founding Member unlock 1-minute polling. Alerts fire within seconds of the poll detecting a matching new email.

Who pays for Gemini and SMS?+

You do — directly. emAIl Sentinel uses your own Gemini API key (free tier gives 1,500 requests/day) and your own SMS provider credentials. The subscription price covers the add-on itself; third-party API costs are between you and the provider.

What happens if I downgrade from Pro?+

Your rules and settings are preserved — we never silently delete your data. Rules above the Free plan limit stop evaluating until you remove some or re-upgrade. Polling is clamped to 15 minutes. Chat and MCP server channels stop firing (but their configuration is kept). Upgrading re-activates everything.

Is the Founding Member price real?+

Yes — $79 one-time for Pro features for the life of the Service, capped at the first 500 purchasers on the Google Workspace Marketplace. After 500 lifetime purchases, the offer is retired and will not return. See Terms § 6.11 for the full commitment.

Can I use it for HIPAA or PCI-regulated email?+

emAIl Sentinel is not designed for regulated data. If you are a HIPAA Covered Entity, PCI merchant, or similar, you may use the Service for non-regulated email only — e.g., general business correspondence that is outside audit scope. See Terms § 2 for the full rule.

How do I refund a purchase?+

Monthly: cancel any time to stop renewals. Annual: 14-day refund window. Founding Member lifetime: 30-day refund window. Email billing@jjjjjenterprises.com with your transaction ID. Full terms in the Terms § 6.6.

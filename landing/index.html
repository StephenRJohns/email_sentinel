How it works

Three steps to never miss again

01

Install in Gmail

Add emAIl Sentinel from the Google Workspace Marketplace in seconds. It lives in your Gmail sidebar — no separate app, no extra login.

02

Write plain-English rules

Tell Sentinel what to watch for in natural language. "Alert me if a client sounds frustrated." Gemini AI handles the interpretation — no code, no regex.

03

Get alerted your way

Alerts arrive via SMS, Google Chat, Calendar event, Sheets row, or Task — the moment a matching message hits your label. You set the channel per rule.

Security & Privacy

Your data stays in your Google account.

No backend. No shared database. No data broker deals. Just your Google account, your Gemini API key, and the alert channels you choose.

🔒

Runs in your Google account

Everything executes in Google Apps Script under your own OAuth grant. We have no backend that sees your mail.

🔑

Encrypted at rest

Credentials, rules, and settings are stored in Google's encrypted UserProperties — private to your script.

🛡️

Annual security review

Completes Google's CASA (Cloud Application Security Assessment) on the cadence Google requires for Gmail restricted scopes.

🌐

GDPR & CCPA ready

Signed Data Processing Addendum available on request. Subprocessor list published in the privacy policy.

Current status

In private pre-launch testing

emAIl Sentinel is currently in private testing with a closed group of users. Public availability via the Google Workspace Marketplace is planned; general sign-ups are not being accepted at this time.

The intended SMS use case is self-notification and small-team alerting — the installing user texts themselves or a small number of colleagues / on-call recipients about email they already have a business reason to see. Users bring their own SMS provider credentials, are the legal sender of every message dispatched on their behalf, and are responsible for TCPA, CAN-SPAM, and carrier-policy compliance under our Terms § 4.1.

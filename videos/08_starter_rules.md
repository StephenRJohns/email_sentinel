# Starter rules
**Duration:** 60 s
**Tool:** Guidde

Tightest possible. Show that you can be alert-ready in 30 seconds
without writing any rule text yourself.

---

## Hook (0:00–0:05)
ON-SCREEN: Rules card → "Starter rules" button.
VOICEOVER:
Skip writing your first rule. Pick from the templates and edit
later.

## Scene 1 — open the picker (0:05–0:18)
ON-SCREEN: Click Starter rules. The card shows the available
templates — Critical security alerts, Payment failures, Customer
escalations, Calendar invites needing response, etc.
VOICEOVER:
Click Starter rules. Each one watches your inbox for a common
pattern with a battle-tested rule text and Calendar as the
default channel.

## Scene 2 — create the set (0:18–0:35)
ON-SCREEN: Click "Create starter rules". The card shows a
confirmation, the new rules appear in the Rules list — all
disabled by default.
VOICEOVER:
Click Create starter rules. They are created in disabled state
so you can edit each one — add SMS recipients, switch the
alert channel, change the watched label — before they start
firing.

## Scene 3 — enable one (0:35–0:50)
ON-SCREEN: Open the "Critical security alerts" rule, add an SMS
recipient, switch the watched label to your real one (e.g.,
INBOX), enable, save.
VOICEOVER:
For each starter rule you want to use, set the Gmail label,
pick your alert channels, and toggle Enable. The starter rule
text is a starting point — refine it once you see real alerts.

## End card (0:50–0:55)
ON-SCREEN: Logo + Marketplace URL
VOICEOVER:
Be alert-ready in thirty seconds. Get e-mail Sentinel — link below.

## Recording checklist (Guidde)

Before you start: Gmail open, demo account, Screenshot mode ON, the
demo account has **no rules yet** (delete any existing rules first
so the Rules card lands cleanly on the empty state), at least one
SMS recipient already saved in Settings, side panel open on the
home card.

1. Hit **Start capture** in the Guidde extension.
2. Click **Rules** on the home card.
3. Click **Starter rules**.
4. (Hover the list of templates for ~2 s — Critical security alerts,
    Payment failures, Customer escalations, Calendar invites needing
    response, etc.)
5. Click **Create starter rules**.
6. Wait for the confirmation; the rules appear in the Rules list,
    all disabled.
7. Click the **Critical security alerts** rule.
8. Scroll to the SMS recipients section.
9. Check at least one SMS recipient.
10. Confirm the Gmail label is **INBOX** (or change it).
11. Toggle **Enable**.
12. Click **Save**.
13. Hit **Stop capture** in Guidde.

After capture: replace the auto-generated voiceover with the
storyboard VOICEOVER lines. Trim aggressively — this video should
feel snappy at under 60 s.
